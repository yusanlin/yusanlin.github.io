Research

My research interests lie in the fields of Information Retrieval, Data Mining, Pattern Recognition and Natural Language Processing. I have combined my knowledge in these fields with my outside interests to form my current research topics. In particular, I'm working with Dr. Heng Xu and Dr. Yilu Zhou on a series of "Fashionology" papers, where we aim to reveal interesting facts from historical fashion data.

Professional Talks

  • Fashionology: A Textual Analysis Approach of Understanding the Fashion Industry, Penn State University Graduate Exhibition, April, 2014 poster

Current Projects

  • Fashion inspiration tracing (with Xu, H. and Zhou, Y.)
  • Fashion trend evolution pattern finding (with Xu, H. and Zhou, Y.)
  • Fashion trend prediction (with Xu, H. and Zhou, Y.)

Fashion Taxonomy

In the process of conducting research, we built a fashion taxonomy. It is a collection of over 2000 fashion-related terms separated into 16 categories. If you're interested in fashion research with text data, this will be a helpful tool for your project. We aim to release it in the first half of 2014.
